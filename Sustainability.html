What We Should Do

  • Reduce energy consumption by using efficient appliances.
  • Adopt eco-friendly transportation methods like cycling or carpooling.
  • Participate in community clean-up and recycling drives.
  • Support policies and initiatives promoting sustainability.
  • Educate others about the importance of a green future.

Ways to Implement Sustainability in Fashion

  • Buy only what you truly need to avoid overconsumption and impulse purchases. Focus on purchasing quality items that can be worn for a long time.
  • Wash clothes in cold water and prefer air drying over electric dryers to save energy and extend the life of your clothing.
  • Upcycle old clothing into something new. For example, turn old T-shirts into reusable bags, add patches to jeans, or transform fabric scraps into accessories.
  • Donate or sell clothing you no longer wear instead of throwing it away. Here are a few places where you can donate:
    • Goodwill
    • ThredUp
    • Poshmark
